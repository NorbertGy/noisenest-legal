Privacy Policy

This app does not collect personally identifiable information.

We use analytics services (such as Firebase) to understand general usage trends. You can disable analytics tracking in the Settings menu.

Advertisements may be shown using AdMob. You can choose whether to allow personalized ads (based on your interests) or only see generic ads.

In-app purchases are handled by the respective app store (Google Play / Apple App Store). We do not store any payment information.

All sound sources are properly credited and used in accordance with their respective licenses. Some sounds are sourced from freesound.org and other free libraries.

This privacy policy may be updated in future versions of the app.
